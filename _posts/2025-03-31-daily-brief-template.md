## What We Are Watching Today

These six stocks have our attention for Monday:

### NVDA ($108.50 premarket)
The chip giant is down another 3% before the open. An analyst downgrade over the weekend cited concerns that AI data center spending might be peaking. The stock is now testing important support at $105. If that breaks, we could see $100 quickly.

**Why it matters:** NVDA moves the whole market these days. What happens here affects the entire tech sector.

### SOFI ($15.22)
Still stuck near recent lows. The fintech lender is trying to hold the $15 level after dropping 53% from its highs. Nothing new over the weekend, but with earnings three weeks away, volatility could pick up.

**Level to watch:** $15.20 needs to hold. Break below and we are looking at $14.

### XOM ($115.40 premarket)
Energy is one of the few green sectors this morning. Exxon is riding higher oil prices and benefiting from rotation out of tech. The stock is breaking above its 50-day moving average.

**Target:** $117 resistance from early March.

### PLTR ($76.50)
Palantir reports earnings Wednesday after the close. Traders are positioning ahead of the report. The stock has been volatile lately, swinging 5% or more daily.

**Risk:** High. This could move 10% either way on earnings.

### JPM ($225.10)
JP Morgan is down slightly with the broader financial sector. Banking stocks have struggled lately as the yield curve flattens. However, JPM remains one of the strongest names in the group.

**Support:** $223 from last week's low.

### COIN ($158.50)
Coinbase is falling with crypto prices this morning. Bitcoin is down 2% overnight. The stock tends to magnify crypto moves, both up and down.

**Note:** Very volatile. Only for traders who can handle big swings.
