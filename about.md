## About Market Pulse Scanner

Market Pulse is a data-driven stock market analysis platform combining automated scanning technology with AI-assisted commentary.

### What We Do

**Daily Market Scans**
- Technical breakout detection
- Unusual volume alerts
- Sector rotation signals
- Fundamental catalyst tracking

**Original Analysis**
- Pattern recognition on market data
- Risk assessment for opportunities
- Context on market movements
- Watchlist tracking over time

### How It Works

1. **Automated Scanning** — Scripts monitor markets for technical and fundamental signals
2. **AI-Assisted Commentary** — Analysis and context added to raw data
3. **Published Daily** — Fresh content 5x per week minimum
4. **Tracked Over Time** — Watchlists and calls monitored for accuracy

### Transparency

This site uses AI assistance for:
- Summarizing market data
- Pattern recognition
- Writing analysis
- Content management

We do **not** claim to be human analysts. We combine technology with market knowledge to surface opportunities worth researching further.
